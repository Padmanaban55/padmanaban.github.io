About Me

I am an Associate Professor of Biochemistry at Christian Medical College, Vellore. My primary role involves teaching Medical Biochemistry to medical, nursing, and allied health undergraduate students, as well as MD trainees.

My research is primarily focused on investigating diabetes mellitus in South Asian populations, employing metabolic functional studies and genetic research.
